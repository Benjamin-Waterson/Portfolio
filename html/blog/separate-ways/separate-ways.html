TRANSCRIPTION: "Separate Ways (Worlds Apart)" - Steve Smith with Journey

I realize that I look a lot of intense stuff on the blog, so let’s calm down with some Steve Smith. I wouldn’t stay this is necessarily easy, but probably more accessible to average drummers. I did this for fun a while back; “Separate Ways” has always been a favorite of my college marching band. Not sure my, but you know how it goes. To be fair, we do have a pretty killer arrangement, stolen from the University of Oregon’s band (we kick ours up a notch to 150 BPM).

We did a Journey show my Sophomore year, and I’ve never been a huge Journey fan; a friend of mine described Journey as “cheese rock”. I can’t say I disagree… but I still like Steve Smith as a drummer and an educator. He’s got some cool moves on the drum solo for this track, so I took a quick look at it. Enjoy.
