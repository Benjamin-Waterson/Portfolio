TRANSCRIPTION: "Odessa" - Navene Koperweis with Animals as Leaders

Posted on 7/2/2020

Today we’re looking at some Animals as Leaders.

I’m analyzing a bit of a deep cut today, from their oft-forgotten second album Weightless (2011). I wasn’t tuned into Animals as Leaders when it came out, and while I suspect it got the attention it deserves when it was first released, it seems to be overlooked now. I guess it doesn’t have the excitement and spectacle of the debut (as badly produced as it was), nor did it establish the trio that we now know as the messiahs of djent and virtuoso playing, the way the recent LP’s have.
