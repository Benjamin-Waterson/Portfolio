About Me

My name is Benjamin Waterson. I am currently in my last year at CU Boulder, studying psychology, music, and TAM (technology, arts, and media). I primarily play drumset, but I also have experience with piano and alto saxophone. I played alto in the CU marching band for 4 years, and I played drumset in the basketball for 4 years as well.
